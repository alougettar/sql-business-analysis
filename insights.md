# Business Insights

## Key Findings
- User growth shows steady increase month over month.
- A small group of users generates a large portion of revenue.
- Churn exists and should be monitored monthly.
- Most active users are on mid-tier plans.

## Recommendations
- Introduce retention strategies for users at risk of churn.
- Focus upsell strategies on high-value customers.
- Monitor churn by subscription plan.
